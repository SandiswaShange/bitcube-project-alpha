## Executive Summary
Sprint 1 focused on enabling employees to book conference rooms and search for suitable rooms without schedule conflicts. The sprint was partially successful because the booking and room filtering functionality was completed, but booking cancellation had to be moved to sprint 2. The sprint highlighted the importance of better estimation, earlier testing, and better sprint tracking.

## Key Accomplishments
- Completed the basic room booking functionality.
- Prevented users from booking rooms that were already occupied during the same time slot.
- Implemented room capacity filtering to help users find suitable meeting rooms.
- Completed all acceptance criteria for stories 1 and 3.

## Challenges Faced
- The team underestimated the complexity of booking validation and conflict prevention.
- Extra testing was required to ensure room scheduling conflicts were prevented correctly.
- Room capacity filtering required additional validation and integration with booking searches.
- Story 4 could not be completed by the end of the sprint.

## Process Improvements Identified
- Improve story point estimation during sprint planning.
- Break large stories into smaller tasks to better track progress.
- Test functionality earlier in the sprint.
- Review sprint progress more often to avoid delays.
