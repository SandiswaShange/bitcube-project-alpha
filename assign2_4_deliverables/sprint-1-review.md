# Sprint 1 Review
Date: 15 May 2026
Sprint Goal: Enable employees to successfully book conference rooms and manage bookings without schedule conflicts

## Sprint Goal Outcome
Partially Met
The sprint goal was partially achieved. Employees are able to search for and book conference rooms while avoiding schedule conflicts, and room filtering functionality was completed. However, booking cancellation functionality was not completed during Sprint 1 and was moved to Sprint 2 due to time constraints and underestimated effort in earlier stories.

## Completed Work

| Story # | Outcome | Acceptance Criteria Met (Yes/No) |
| Story #1 Basic room booking | Employees can book conference rooms successfully without schedule conflicts | Yes |
| Story #3 Room capacity filtering | Employees can filter available rooms by seating capacity | Yes |

## Incomplete Work

| Story # | Reason | Decision (Carry / Split / Drop) |
| Story #4 Booking cancellation | The team underestimated the effort needed for Stories 1 and 3. More time was spent implementing booking logic, testing room availability logic, and integrating room capacity filtering with search functionality. As a result, we were unable to complete story 4. | Carry |

## Stakeholder Feedback

Positive feedback:
- The booking process was straightforward and easy to understand.

Concerns or questions:
- Users wanted a clearer confirmation that a booking had been successfully saved.
- There was concern about how cancellations would work since the feature was not yet available.
- Questions were raised about whether users would receive notifications when bookings changed.

Requests or adjustments:
- Add booking cancellation in Sprint 2.
- Consider notifications for room booking changes in a future sprint.
