## Total Story Points
Total Story Points = 66
## Proposed Team Velocity
A realistic Agile team velocity would typically be between 10-20 story points per sprint in two-week sprints. I choose 15 for the purpose of this project.
## Estimated Number of Sprints
66 ÷ 15 ≈ 5 sprints required 
## Reasoning
The team includes developers, testers, and a Scrum Master working in two-week sprints. Complex stories such as recurring meetings, admin dashboards, and reporting require more development and testing effort. The lower-complexity stories such as cancellation and filtering can be completed faster and help balance sprint workloads so they are addressed in the first sprint. The project is divided so that the core booking functionality is completed first, followed by administration, reporting, and advanced scheduling features.
